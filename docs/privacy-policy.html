Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 22, 2026

The Veil is developed by Ni Biashara LLC. This policy describes how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

The Veil collects the following data, stored locally on your device only:

  • Gameplay analytics — puzzle completion times, play patterns, and behavioral preferences. This data is used to personalize your in-game experience (e.g., AI companion behavior).
  • Save data — your game progress, including day number, level, coins, and upgrades.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any personal data to third parties. All analytics data remains on your device unless you explicitly opt into cloud features in a future update.

Advertising

The Veil uses in-world (diegetic) advertising. In the initial release, all ad content is fictional placeholder content. When real advertising is introduced, we will update this policy and request consent.

Children's Privacy

The Veil does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game does not require account creation or personal data entry.

Data Storage & Security

All data is stored in your device's private application storage, protected by your device's security mechanisms. No data is transmitted over the network in the current version.

Your Rights

You can delete all stored data at any time by clearing the app's data in your device settings, or by using the in-game "Delete Save" option.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the game evolves. Changes will be posted here with an updated effective date.
