FAQ

Q: How do I reset my progress?
A: Go to your device settings, find The Veil, and clear app data. Or use the in-game menu.

Q: The game is running slowly.
A: Try closing other apps and restarting the game. The Veil is optimized for most devices.

Q: I lost my save data.
A: Save data is stored locally on your device. If you uninstall the app, save data may be lost. Cloud saves are planned for a future update.
